ABOUT US

The ultimate solution to the stateless polulation
- a language learning center for the refugees of Bangladesh.

Global population crisis

The Population of stateless people in this world has risen to 65.6 million by the end of 2016, which is more than the population of United Kingdom. -Un Refugee Agency; UNHCR.

On 28th September 2018, at the 73rd UN General Assembly the Prime Minister of Bangladesh stated “There are 1.1 million Rohingya refugees now in Bangladesh”. Criterion learning center will host foreign NGO workers, refugee helping groups and any foreign individuals who are willing to teach the children at the Rohingya camps in Bangladesh. As a result, those children who are seeking shelter in Bangladesh can achieve knowledge through different cultures.

In the future, these children may become multi language skilled workers . Hence, the refugees are no more a burden, rather a contributor to our GDP.
